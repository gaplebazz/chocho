Our Story

A Palace of Timeless
Rajasthani Elegance

Founded in the spirit of Swaraj — self-rule and dignity — Hotel Swaraj was born from the vision of celebrating India's most regal city through an experience that is authentically Rajasthani at its heart.

Every corridor is adorned with hand-painted frescoes, every room draped in hand-blocked fabrics from the bazaars of Sanganer. We weave local craftsmanship into every comfort, offering you not just a stay, but a living museum of Rajputana heritage.

Nestled near the historic walled city, we are moments from the Hawa Mahal, Amber Fort, and the vibrant Johari Bazaar — yet a world apart in serenity.
